Privacy Policy

This policy applies to Identity Wallet.

Data Collection

Identity Wallet does not collect personal data on a developer server in the current local-first MVP.

On-Device Sources

If you grant access, the app may use on-device photo metadata, current location availability, and contact metadata counts to create local Open Wallet draft suggestions. These summaries stay on the device.

No Account or Tracking

The current app does not require an account, does not include third-party analytics, and does not use data for tracking.

Sharing

When you use the QR/share flow, only the Open Wallet payload and preview fields you choose to share are included in the shared link. Closed Wallet content is not included in the shared payload.

Contact

For questions or support, use the support issue tracker.
